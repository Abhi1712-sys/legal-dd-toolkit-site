Executive Summary

This due diligence report has been prepared for ABC Technologies Private Limited in connection with a proposed investment transaction. The review focuses on corporate compliance, regulatory filings, employment practices, IP ownership, and litigation exposure.

Scope of Review

  • Corporate & secretarial compliance
  • Tax & regulatory compliance
  • Employment & labour laws
  • Intellectual property
  • Litigation & disputes

Key Findings

Area Observation Risk Level
Corporate Delayed ROC filings Medium
Labour Missing employment contracts High
IP No IP assignment deeds High

Red Flags

  • Absence of IP ownership documentation
  • Non-compliance with POSH requirements

Conclusion

Subject to resolution of the identified high-risk issues, the transaction may proceed with appropriate safeguards.
